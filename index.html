The Three Hansen Parameters

A comprehensive framework for understanding molecular interactions through three distinct force components.

δD

Dispersion Forces

London dispersion interactions arising from temporary dipoles in molecules. These forces are present in all molecules and form the baseline of molecular cohesion.

δP

Polar Forces

Permanent dipole-dipole interactions. These forces are significant in polar molecules and directly affect solubility behavior.

δH

Hydrogen Bonding

Specific hydrogen bond interactions involving hydrogen atoms bonded to electronegative atoms. The strongest of the three parameters.
